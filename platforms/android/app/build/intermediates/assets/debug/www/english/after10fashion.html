Fashion designing

Fashion design is not just about designing clothes! There are different disciplines and fields present within the fashion industry. Apparel Design, Leather Design, Fashion Communication, Textiles, Manufacturing, Sales, Retail Merchandising, Fashion Photography, Fashion Event Management etc are some of the well known parts of fashion industry. Many fashion industry courses are available in India, which focuses on the above mentioned disciplines.

Modern Indian society has become more style conscious. Thanks to the internet, movies and television, people have started following latest trends in the World of fashion! They are even willing to spend more and more on clothes and apparels!

All these factors indicate that the fashion industry will have a bright future in India. A bright future means that more job opportunities will be generated by this industry.
