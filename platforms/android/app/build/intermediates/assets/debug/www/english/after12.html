Here we are going to provide career counselling guidance for the students who wish to pursue their career after 12th. There are lacs of students in our country who are puzzled regarding their career after schooling. Many students search a lot related to career after 12th in the field of Science stream/ Commerce stream/ Arts stream.

1. Science Stream (PCM) :

PCM stands for Physics, chemistry, Maths. Most of the students who are having PCM in 12th standard, they ask questions about the courses available other than engineering. Here you can check the career counselling material which will clear all of your doubts related to science stream.

So you are a student of science and you have done your 10+2 with PCB (Biology). Now you have the opportunity to choose the right career. You can choose the medical field as your area of interest. 3. COMMERCE:

“Commerce is a branch of any business which covers the economical part of the legal, social, political, technological and cultural system”. Commerce courses are the choice of a big community of the students who persue commerce as main subject in 12th. Commerce count the all activities those are involved in the exchange of goods and services considered to the money, between the person and companies.

4. ARTS:

Arts stream is one of the most diverse fields among the trio viz. Arts, Science and Commerce. There are innumerable courses after 12th Arts in India. Be it animation, law, journalism, humanities, management, fashion or textile design, aviation, banking or any other professional course, an Arts student will have a lot of career options to choose from.
